Session introduction of COSCUP 2014

The main theme of COSCUP 2014 is designated as : Make Things Happen

2014 is a year that software comes closer the people's life. We can see Open sourced hardware and software makes the Maker community blooming and new style of civic movements that powered by Open Data and social media. We focus on Open Source Maker, Open Governance and Open Data. But we don't miss important topics like community culture and legal issues. COSCUP is a wonderful platform for both Open Source Users, Coders, and Promoters.

COSCUP 2014

COSCUP is the largest annual FLOSS conference organized by the local communities in Taiwan. The conference includes sessions for users, enthusiastic promoters, software coders or anyone who is interested in cutting-edge FLOSS technologies. Our goal is to create a friendly and informative environment for people from different communities to make friends, to learn new technologies, and to get inspired by each other.
